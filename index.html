Our Mission

We understand that gaps in foundational learning can hold a student back. TRY Literacy focuses exclusively on diagnosing and closing those gaps in a supportive, virtual environment. We give middle and elementary school students the tools they need to succeed in higher grades.

Proven Results

Our Core Instruction Areas

Reading Comprehension

We help students move past decoding and focus on what they are reading. Skills taught include:

  • Identifying the Main Idea and Supporting Details
  • Making Inferences and Predictions
  • Understanding Text Structure and Vocabulary in Context

Basic Math Skills

We build confidence by strengthening the essential math building blocks necessary for Algebra and geometry:

  • Mastery of Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division
  • Fraction and Decimal Concepts
  • Introduction to Pre-Algebraic Thinking and Problem Solving

Who We Serve

TRY Literacy specializes in the critical developmental period when foundational skills determine future academic success.

Grades 3 - 5

Focus on building strong basic math fact fluency and transitioning from "learning to read" to "reading to learn."

Grades 6 - 8

Emphasis on closing complex fraction/decimal gaps and developing critical thinking skills needed for middle school texts.
